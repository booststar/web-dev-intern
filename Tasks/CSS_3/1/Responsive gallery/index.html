VADA PAV

Vada Pav is a popular street food in Maharashtra. It consists of a spicy potato fritter (vada) sandwiched between a pav (soft bread roll), served with chutneys.

KOTHAMBIR VADI

Kothimbir Vadi is a savory snack made from besan (gram flour), coriander leaves, and spices. It's steamed or fried and then sliced into pieces before serving.

MODAK

Modak is a sweet dumpling often associated with Lord Ganesha. It has a sweet filling made from coconut and jaggery, encased in a rice flour or wheat flour shell.

PURAN POLI

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread stuffed with a mixture of chana dal (split Bengal gram), jaggery, and spices. It is often enjoyed during festivals like Holi and Ganesh Chaturth

POHE

Poha is a popular breakfast dish made from flattened rice cooked with mustard seeds, turmeric, curry leaves, and garnished with peanuts and coriander.

MISAL

Misal Pav is a spicy curry made with sprouted moth beans or matki, topped with farsan (crispy snacks) and served with pav. It's a flavorful and hearty breakfast or snack.
